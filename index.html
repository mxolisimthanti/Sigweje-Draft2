Welcome to Sigweje High

Sigweje High School, established in 1996, serves Grades 8–12 in Ladysmith, KwaZulu-Natal near GG Shopping Centre. Our mission is to nurture excellence in academics, sport, and culture.

ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE

We hold a proud tradition of Academic excellence across a variety of subjects and are consistently rated as one of the top feeder schools for many of the major universities. Our state-of-the-art academic and classroom resources help enable one of the best academic environments for South African schools.

SPORTING SUCCESSES

Northcliff boasts an outstanding record of extramural excellence and participation. With a legacy of achieving major successes across many sporting codes, we continue to triumph in, and develop the traditional sports that we offer to our students.
